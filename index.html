Welcome to Little Flower School, Sabarmati

At Little Flower School, Sabarmati, Ahmedabad, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering an environment where students excel academically, socially, and emotionally. Established in 1978, our school has a proud legacy of delivering high-quality education and shaping future leaders who are compassionate, responsible, and driven by curiosity.

About Us

Little Flower School is more than just an educational institution. It is a community where learning is celebrated, values are instilled, and dreams are encouraged. Located in the heart of Sabarmati, Ahmedabad, our school blends traditional values with modern teaching methods to provide an enriching educational experience. We believe in holistic development and focus on equipping students with the skills and knowledge required to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision and Mission

Vision:
>> To create a learning environment where every child feels valued, respected, and inspired to achieve their full potential.

Mission:
>> To impart quality education that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.
>> To nurture moral values, discipline, and respect for diversity.
>> To foster a sense of responsibility towards society and the environment.



Academics

At Little Flower School, we follow a well-rounded curriculum that aligns with Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board [GSEB]. Our dedicated and highly qualified faculty ensures that students receive personalized attention and excel in all academic disciplines.

Primary Education: Focused on foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving through engaging and interactive learning methods.

Middle School: Broadens knowledge with a focus on science, mathematics, languages, and social studies, while introducing project-based learning.

High School: Prepares students for board examinations with rigorous academic preparation and guidance for future career pathways.

Facilities

Little Flower School is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to enhance the learning experience:
>> Smart classrooms with interactive teaching aids.
>> Fully equipped science and computer laboratories.
>> A well-stocked library with a wide range of books and digital resources.
>> Spacious playgrounds and sports equipment.
>> Dedicated spaces for arts, music, and dance activities.
>> Safe and secure premises with CCTV surveillance and trained staff.
